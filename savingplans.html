Saving Plans

Savings Plans is a discount plan that offers lower pay-as-you-go pricing. In exchange, you need to prepay the committed usage amount.

For example, if you commit to spending ¥1,000 per year and prepay upfront for 3 years, your standard subscription price will be calculated at a 40% discount. The price for real-time weather is reduced from ¥0.001 per request to ¥0.0004 per request. This means you get approximately 2.5 million requests with a 3-year saving plan.

Commitment Amount

The commitment amount is the amount you commit to using within one year. This amount expires after 1 year or when fully used.

Commitment Terms

  • 1-year: Total upfront cost = Commitment Amount x 1 year
  • 3-year: Total upfront cost = Commitment Amount x 3 years

Saving Rate

Saving Rate is the discount multiplier applied for pay-as-you-go pricing.
